Exeggutor

Type:

Exeggutor (Japanese: ナッシー Nassy) is a dual-type Grass/Psychic Pokémon introduced in Generation I. Exeggutor has a dual-type Grass/Dragon regional form. It evolves from Exeggcute when exposed to a Leaf Stone. All Exeggcute in Alola evolve into this form regardless of their origin.

Ditto

Type:

Normal

Ditto (Japanese: メタモン Metamon) is a Normal-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is not known to evolve into or from any other Pokémon. Ditto is well known for its unique ability to breed with any Pokémon other than the Pokémon in the No Eggs Discovered group and other Ditto, including some gender-unknown Pokémon, to produce Eggs of the other's species.
