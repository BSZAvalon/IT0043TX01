On May 4 and 5, 2024

We hope this message finds you well. Due to the extraordinary heatwave currently affecting our region, we have made the decision to cancel all classes for May 4 and 5. Thank you for your understanding and cooperation during this time. Stay safe, and we look forward to resuming normal operations soon.

Keep Safe, Tamaraw
